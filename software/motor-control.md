# Motor Control Logic

## Movement Functions

The robot uses motor control functions to:

- Move forward
- Move backward
- Turn left
- Turn right
- Brake

## Control Purpose

Movement decisions are based on sensor readings. When an obstacle is detected, the robot can stop or change direction. When waste is detected, it can stop and begin the collection process.

## Notes

Motor speed and turning behaviour may need tuning depending on surface friction, chassis balance, and battery level.
